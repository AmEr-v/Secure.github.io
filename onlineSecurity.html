Understanding Online Threats

Every time you connect to the internet, you are exposed to a variety of threats such as phishing attacks, identity theft, malware, scams, and data breaches. Building good online habits helps you stay protected and keeps your private information safe.

üîí Essential Online Safety Practices

  • Use Strong, Unique Passwords:
    Create passwords that are long (at least 12 characters), random, and unique for each account. Use a password manager like Bitwarden or 1Password to manage them safely.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):
    Even if someone steals your password, 2FA protects your accounts by requiring a second step (like a code from your phone or an authenticator app) to log in.
  • Think Before You Click:
    Hover over links before clicking to see where they really go. Avoid clicking suspicious links in emails, text messages, or on social media.
  • Use Secure Websites:
    Only enter personal or payment information on websites that have HTTPS (look for the padlock symbol in the address bar).
  • Limit Social Media Sharing:
    Avoid oversharing personal information (birthdate, address, travel plans) on social platforms. Hackers use this data for identity theft and targeted attacks.
  • Adjust Privacy Settings:
    Review the privacy settings on your social media, email, and Google account. Restrict who can see your posts, location, and contact info.
  • Keep Devices Updated:
    Update your phone, computer, browsers, and apps as soon as updates become available to fix security vulnerabilities.
  • Use Antivirus and Anti-Malware Tools:
    Programs like Malwarebytes and Bitdefender can detect and remove threats from infected downloads or malicious sites.
  • Be Careful with Public Wi-Fi:
    Avoid logging into sensitive accounts (like online banking) when using public Wi-Fi unless you‚Äôre protected by a VPN.
  • Backup Your Data:
    Regularly backup your important files to an external hard drive or encrypted cloud storage to protect against ransomware or accidental loss.
